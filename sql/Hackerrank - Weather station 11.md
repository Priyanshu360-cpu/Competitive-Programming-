# Solution - MySql
```
SELECT DISTINCT CITY FROM STATION WHERE NOT (CITY LIKE '%A' OR CITY LIKE '%E' OR CITY LIKE '%I' OR CITY LIKE '%O' OR CITY LIKE '%U') OR NOT (CITY LIKE 'A%' OR CITY LIKE 'E%' OR CITY LIKE 'I%' OR CITY LIKE 'O%' OR CITY LIKE 'U%');
```
